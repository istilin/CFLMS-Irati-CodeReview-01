At a very early age I developed an interest in science. I think I was around 5 when I wanted to become an astronaut (surprise surprise). During my highschool years I realised that is a very tough goal to achieve so I lowered my expectations and goals a little. I developed an interest in astronomy for a long time so I decided to study astronomy. Since that wasn't possible in Spain, I had to start studying physics in Bilbao, although I was more into maths. After my first year of University, I came across this astronomy camp called IAYC which was the beginning of one of my most important journeys in my life. There I got to learn a lot about research, astronomy, I improved my English, I became more social, I learned so much about different cultures, etc. After a couple of years of tears and pain at university, and when my motivation level wasn't at the greatest moment, I was in Tenerife on holidays with my parents, and we decided to visit the University there. We realised that I could change to Physics there and do some real astronomy subjects so within 2 weeks I was enrolled there and ready to move to the island! My life changed completely and my motivation level increased again!
